Get to know Richard Banyard

Richard Banyard took a typical route into engineering, studying control and automation at Sheffield before enrolling onto ABB’s graduate scheme. During his 10 years with ABB, Richard worked in a variety of roles from project engineering, internal sales and technical management.

Since buying Op-tec Systems Richard has relocated and grown the business year-on-year. Keeping the focus on client outcomes, Richard continues to build on a strong foundation of robust processes, broad expertise and deep understanding of the pressures facing UK industry.
