Op-tec provides its clients with skilled resources and knowledge whenever they need them, usually on a project basis, although we have become a key engineering partner to several smaller manufacturers, where an in-house resource is too costly.

When implementing upgrades and expansions to existing systems, Op-tec’s engineers adapt their programming style to match the existing configuration for ease of maintenance and support.

A flexible and personalised service helps to ensure a clear understanding of the project requirements, including addressing the clients' wider business needs.

Op-tec’s senior engineers have broad systems engineering capabilities and proven track records in delivering innovative solutions that meet and often exceed expectations.

The tight integration of key system components improves system reliability and reduces maintenance costs.

Op-tec works closely with each Client to implement projects “right first-time”, on time and within budget.

The continuity of project management and engineering from conceptual design through to commissioning helps to ensure optimum performance throughout the life of each application.

Op-tec aims to build lasting relationships with all of its Clients.

In the case of companies whose process plant does not support extensive control & instrumentation expertise, Op-tec offers the economic solution of "part-time" specialist support.
