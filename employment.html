OP-TEC Systems is an independent process automation of control systems consultancy and systems integrator undertaking the engineering, supply and commissioning of process control and data acquisition systems for the process and manufacturing industries. Our engineers experience the full life cycle of control systems development from conceptual design studies, through detailed system design and application engineering, to on-site commissioning and operator training.

We are seeking professional engineers for the following positions:

  • Senior DCS, PLC & SCADA Engineer
  • Junior DCS, PLC & SCADA Engineer

Successful candidates will have the following experience and skill set:

  • Ability to work with minimal supervision in the office and during client-site-based assignments
  • Excellent organisational and communication skills
  • Enthusiasm and the ability to learn quickly
  • Excellent administrative, organisational and communication skills
  • Ability to interface with internal and client personnel at all levels

Previous experience should include process control and/or real-time software development where applicable.

If you have the necessary skills and feel that you would thrive in a dynamic, small company environment, please email your CV to: jobs@op-tec.co.uk.
