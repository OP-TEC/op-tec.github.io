Founded in 1996, Op-tec Systems is an independent provider of customised process automation and data acquisition solutions for the process and manufacturing industries.

The company provides cost-effective engineering services covering conceptual design, specification, project management, hardware and software design, assembly, configuration, programming, testing, installation, commissioning, operator training and technical support.

By viewing each project from the End-User's perspective we aim to make a positive contribution to your business by providing:

  • Effective automation solutions for improved product quality and energy efficiency
  • Innovative solutions to unusual and/or complex manufacturing problems
  • Open access to process information for business management and control
  • Flexibility for easy integration with other equipment
  • Quality documentation for process validation, environmental compliance and maintenance
  • A dependable systems partnership
  • Long term commitment and support.

Working closely with your own project team, our engineers provide a flexible and personalised service tailored to each application, including the evolution of legacy DCS (distributed control system), PLC (programmable logic controller) and SCADA (supervisory control and data acquisition) systems into current generation solutions for process management and control.

Where practical process control, manufacturing automation, supervisory control, data acquisition and management information systems are seamlessly integrated into a total automation solution.
