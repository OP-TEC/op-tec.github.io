PROCESS CONTROL SYSTEM SERVICES

Op-tec Systems offer a complete range of project engineering and consultancy services, supplying users of industrial automation and information systems in the process and manufacturing industries.

We specialise in using control systems to increase process efficiency, providing cost-effective engineering services covering:

  • Conceptual design and specification
  • Obsolescence planning
  • Project management
  • Hardware and software design
  • Assembly and configuration
  • Application programming and testing
  • Installation and commissioning
  • Training and coaching
  • Technical support and on-going service


  Control Specialists

Our skilled project teams bring a depth of application experience, having implemented advanced control and batch solutions across a wide variety of industries.

We resource projects according to your needs not our scheduling constraints.

  Effective Technology

We believe the way to make industrial programmable systems secure, efficient, and maintainable; is elegant design and effective matching of technologies.

Op-tec approaches projects aiming to reduce complexity and utilise open standards.
