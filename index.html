Are you a manufacturer?

Losing orders and bleeding profits?

Chances are it’s because you need better control of your business

Which common issues apply to you?


  • Profit margins not where you'd like them
    Increasing input and operational costs, price-focused clients and competitors with newer facilities are putting pressure on many UK manufacturers. You're not alone, but you do have the choice to do something about it.
  • Unexpected and unplanned shutdowns
    Disruptions cost money, you know they can be avoided but pressure to keep production up encourage you to delay maintenance activities.
  • The business can’t support large capital investments
    Rather than being reliable revenue generators your major assets are old and unreliable, but the cost of replacement has to keep getting pushed into 'next year'.
  • Staff not motivated impacting performance
    People can't get engaged in their work if they see no connection between the attention they pay to their task and the greater good. We can deliver productivity information down to individual lines, machines or staff members.
  • Go home fearful of a call from the nightshift
    When you left work, things were running smoothly, but for how long? An intelligence-led predictive maintenance regime would reduce outages, and give warning of pending issues.
  • Tired of work getting in the way of family life
    It's hard not to let worries about business performance interfere with other parts of your day. Having real-time information on the performance of your company's assets at all times may help.

If any of this feels like your situation you may be putting your businesses performance down to the current climate, believing that if you can make it through this period things will get better.

In which case, there's some bad news and some good news:

The bad news first...

Continuing to do more of the same is not a sustainable strategy, neither is waiting for the current situation to change (it could move in a direction that makes things worse, not better).

Now the good news...

You can take a targeted approach to investing, making your business less reliant on external factors. Equip yourself and your teams to make better data-driven decisions whether they are on an operational or strategic level.

We have experience helping UK manufacturers unlock the value within their operations:

  • Present the right people with the right information at the right time
  • Plan the digitisation of your processes to reduce costs and increase flexibility
  • Improve plant profitability through better use of your existing control systems
  • Secure your company's future using a risk-based approach to safety and security
  • Give your workforce real-time information on their performance and productivity

We help clients across a range of industries. As an independent, service-orientated business we take pride in recommending the solution that best supports your business objectives.

You have a choice: continue as you are, hoping that things will get better "when the economy picks up" or start your company's transition today, to improve resilience, flexibility and gain the competitive edge in the digital age.
