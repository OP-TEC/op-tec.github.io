Security

Pragmatic risk management for security


Survey the estate. Identify the systems are that are most critical according to their vulnerability and importance to the operation.

Check that firewalls are correctly configured and switched on.

Maintain operating systems, firmware and programs to the latest compatible versions with all available security patches.

Create a recovery plan for the event of cyber-attack. Maintain up-to-date backups from all controllers, with copies to be held in secure storage remotely and on site.
