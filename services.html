Op-tec Systems offers a complete range of project engineering and consultancy services in the fields of industrial automation and information systems to the process and manufacturing industries. Through core services of technical consultancy, system design, application programming, systems integration and project management, we provide our clients with a total solution, from initial concept to commissioning and on-going support.

Our wide ranging experience across a wide spectrum of industries and applications enable us to provide substantial input to control, instrumentation and data processing projects, ensuring optimum performance and support throughout the life of the application.

The commitment of the Company and the dedication & expertise of our staff, give us the confidence to successfully develop and implement diverse automation solutions for our clients.

Typical Company services include:

  • Site investigation and system audits
  • Consultancy and feasibility studies
  • Preparation of User Requirement and Functional Design Specifications
  • Control system and vendor selection
  • Project management
  • Control panel design and systems engineering
  • Control system assembly and checkout
  • System integration and testing
  • Application software design and implementation
  • Programmable logic controllers (PLC)
  • Human/machine interfaces (HMI)
  • Distributed control systems (DCS)
  • Supervisory control and data acquisition (SCADA) systems
  • Condition monitoring systems
  • Energy monitoring systems
  • Real-time process data reporting systems
  • Plant-wide data historian systems
  • Management information systems (MIS)
  • Reverse engineering of existing applications
  • Bespoke software solutions
  • Pre-commissioning, commissioning and site trials
  • Control system validation
  • Customised product and application training
  • Control system upgrades and expansions
  • Support and maintenance of automation and control systems supplied by ourselves and others.
  • Upgrade and replacement of obsolete control & monitoring systems
  • DCS, PLC and SCADA software conversion (e.g. "reverse engineering" of legacy systems and configuration of original application in current-generation system)
  • Provision of an "on-call" extension to the Client's own C&I and MIS engineering resources.
  • Supply and support of OPsCon replacement console for Bailey Infi and Net 90 DCSs.
