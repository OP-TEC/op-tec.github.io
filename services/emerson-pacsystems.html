PACSystems™ by Emerson

Emerson’s PACSystems™ represent a range of high performance process control and automation products which were previously supported by GE. These products are used in a range of industries to provide a level of performance and interoperability usually associated with distributed control systems. Features such as high availability, hazardous area certification and advanced process control make PACSystems™ products suitable for the most demanding applications.

A broad range of products form the PACSystems™ ecosystem. Op-tec Systems has experience with the following:

  • PAC8000 Remote I/O
  • RX3i PLCs
